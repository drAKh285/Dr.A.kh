Frequently Asked Questions

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in starts at 2:00 PM. Check-out must be done before 12:00 PM. If you require early check-in or late check-out, you can speak to reception to arrange this subject to availability.

Is there parking at the hotel?

Yes, there is a parking space for guests. Parking is available for an additional fee or may be free depending on hotel policy. It is recommended to ask at the time of booking to ensure availability.

Is Wi-Fi available in the rooms?

Yes, Wi-Fi is free throughout the hotel including the rooms. You can get the password upon check-in.

Can I order room service?

Yes, the hotel offers 24-hour room service. You can order food or drinks by calling the reception or through the in-room dining menu.

Does the hotel offer airport shuttle services?

Yes, we offer airport shuttle services. You can book this service in advance through the reception for an additional fee.

Does the hotel have a swimming pool?

Yes, the hotel has a swimming pool. You can enjoy it during the specified operating hours. Be sure to check the pool hours at the front desk.

Is there a fitness center at the hotel?

Yes, there is a fully equipped fitness center. You can use it during business hours. Be sure to check with the front desk for opening hours.

Does the hotel offer daily housekeeping?

Yes, rooms are cleaned daily. If you require additional cleaning or any special service, you can contact the reception.

Can I bring pets to the hotel?

It depends on the hotel policy. Some hotels allow pets for an additional fee, while others do not. It is best to check the hotel policy when booking.

Is there a restaurant in the hotel?

Yes, there is a restaurant in the hotel that serves a variety of local and international dishes. You can check the menu in the room or ask the reception for opening hours.

Can I organize events or meetings at the hotel?

Yes, we offer meeting and event services in designated rooms. You can contact the reception for more details about booking and available services.

Does the hotel have a playroom or entertainment areas for children?

Yes, we have a dedicated children's area with fun activities and games. You can ask at the reception for details.

Does the hotel offer treatment or spa services?

Yes, we have a spa centre offering a range of relaxing treatments such as massage and skin care. You can book through the reception or over the phone.

Can I have connecting rooms?

Yes, we do offer connecting rooms upon request, but this is subject to availability at the time you book. It is advisable to inform us in advance to accommodate your request.

Does the room have a safe to store valuables?

Yes, all rooms are equipped with safes to store valuables. If you need assistance in using them, please feel free to contact the reception.

Does the hotel have facilities for people with disabilities?

Yes, we have rooms and facilities for people with disabilities such as lifts, wide doors and equipped bathrooms. It is advisable to inform us in advance to arrange the appropriate room.

Can I rent a bike or a car from the hotel?

Yes, the hotel offers bike and car rental. You can get details and prices upon check-in or contact the reception.

Are there any additional fees when paying by credit card?

There are no additional fees when paying by credit card. We only charge based on the actual cost of accommodation and services.

Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can modify or cancel my reservation, but some reservations may be non-refundable depending on the hotel policy. It is best to check the conditions when booking.

Can I get directions to local attractions?

Of course, we can provide information about local attractions and the best things to do. You can speak to the reception for advice and maps.

Can I send mail or parcels from the hotel?

Yes, you can send mail and parcels through the reception. We offer local and international mail services for an additional fee.
